Biggie — Brooklyn's king of flow and storytelling.

Regional Influence

  • East Coast: Born in NYC. Lyricism, hunger, grit. Think Nas, Biggie, Wu-Tang.
  • West Coast: Laid-back funk and raw realness. Dre, Snoop, N.W.A, Tupac.
  • South: Bounce and trap domination. Outkast, Lil Wayne, Future.
  • Midwest: Creativity and speed. Kanye, Common, Bone Thugs, Twista.

Major Moments in Hip Hop

1973: Birth of Hip Hop

DJ Kool Herc throws a Bronx party and loops breakbeats — the night that changed music forever.

1986: Rock Meets Rap

Run-DMC and Aerosmith drop “Walk This Way” — taking hip hop global.

1990s: East vs West

Biggie vs. Tupac, Death Row vs. Bad Boy — pride, poetry, and pain.

2000s: South Rises

ATL takes over — Outkast, T.I., Lil Jon, crunk, snap, trap.

2010s: Conscious Meets Commercial

Drake, Kendrick, Cole — melody, message, momentum.

2020s: TikTok & AI Influence

Socials break songs. AI mimics flows. But the streets still set the tone.
